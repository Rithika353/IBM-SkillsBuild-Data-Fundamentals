# Introduction to Data Cleaning

## What is Data Cleaning?

Data cleaning is the process of identifying and correcting errors, inconsistencies, and inaccuracies in a dataset. Clean data improves the quality of analysis and leads to more reliable business decisions.

## Why Data Cleaning is Important

- Improves data accuracy
- Removes duplicate records
- Handles missing values
- Standardizes formats
- Enhances the reliability of reports and dashboards

## Example

Before Cleaning:

| Customer | City      |
|----------|-----------|
| John     | Hyderabad |
| John     | Hyderabad |
| Sarah    | NULL      |

After Cleaning:

| Customer | City      |
|----------|-----------|
| John     | Hyderabad |
| Sarah    | Bengaluru |
