# Module Summary

In this module, I learned:

- What data is and why it matters.
- The difference between data and information.
- Different types of data.
- Structured, semi-structured, and unstructured data.
- The stages of the data lifecycle.
- The concept of Big Data and its applications.
- The standard data analytics process.

## Key Takeaways

- Data is the foundation of analytics.
- Clean data leads to accurate insights.
- Visualization helps communicate results effectively.
- Data-driven decisions improve business outcomes.
