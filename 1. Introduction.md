# Introduction to Data

## What is Data?

Data is a collection of raw facts, numbers, text, images, videos, or observations that have not yet been processed into meaningful information.

Examples:
- Student marks
- Product prices
- Customer names
- Website clicks
- Temperature readings

Data is the foundation of every business decision. Organizations collect data to understand customers, improve products, optimize operations, and predict future trends.

---

## Why is Data Important?

Data helps organizations:

- Make informed decisions
- Understand customer behavior
- Identify trends
- Improve efficiency
- Reduce business risks
- Increase revenue

---

## Real-World Example

An online shopping platform collects:

- Customer name
- Purchased products
- Order value
- Payment method
- Delivery location

By analyzing this data, the company can recommend products, optimize inventory, and improve customer satisfaction.
