# Duplicate Data

Duplicate records occur when the same information appears multiple times.

## Problems

- Incorrect analysis
- Inflated totals
- Misleading reports

## Example

| Order ID | Customer |
|----------|----------|
| 1001 | Alice |
| 1001 | Alice |

After removing duplicates:

| Order ID | Customer |
|----------|----------|
| 1001 | Alice |

## Best Practices

- Check unique IDs
- Remove exact duplicates
- Review partial duplicates carefully
